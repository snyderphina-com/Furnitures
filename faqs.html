These products are available in various designs to accommodate the diverse usage habits of different countries and meet a wide range of needs.

What is the expertise of FurnitureHub Group?

✨ FurmitureHub, originally from Nairobi and established in 1989, is the only household items manufacturer in the industry with a fully automated and integrated production line. What are your payment terms?

The standard payment method is to pay a 30% deposit by M-pesa,Paypal or Cash upon order confirmation, and settle the remaining 70% balance before shipment. ✨

What is your standard packaging method?

✨The typical packaging method is to place one product in a brown box, which can be customized with printed logos or affixed with standard-sized color labels. If there are any other specific packaging requirements, we can provide recommendations and suggest the best solution based on our experience.
